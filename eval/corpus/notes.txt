LECTURE NOTES: COSMIC PLANT EXPERIMENT

In 2023, Dr. Elena Markovic conducted an experiment at the University of Pristina Biology Lab.

She discovered a fictional plant called "Lunaria Nova" which only grows under artificial blue light combined with classical music by Beethoven.

The plant was placed in three environments:
1. A dark room with no sound
2. A greenhouse with natural sunlight
3. A controlled lab with blue LED lights and Beethoven’s Symphony No. 7

After 14 days, only the third environment showed growth. The plant reached a height of 23 cm and emitted a faint green glow at night.

Dr. Markovic concluded that sound frequency might influence plant growth, but the results were marked as “inconclusive due to lack of replication.”

A follow-up experiment was planned for 2024 using Mozart compositions instead of Beethoven.

IMPORTANT FINDING:
The plant reacts negatively to heavy metal music, causing it to stop growing within 48 hours.
